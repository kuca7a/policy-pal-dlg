Simplifying accident documentation

Policy Pal is your trusted companion for accident documentation and insurance information exchange. Be prepared for unexpected incidents with our quick and secure QR code solution.

To get started with Policy Pal, the first step is to sign up for an account. During the registration process, you'll need to provide your email address and create a secure password. Don't forget to review and agree to the terms and conditions. After completing these steps, tap "Sign Up" to create your account. If you already have an account, you can easily log in. Open the app and select "Log In." Enter your registered email address and the password associated with your account, and then tap "Log In" to access your account. Upon logging in, you'll be prompted to provide your personal details. This includes your full name, contact information, and insurance details. It's essential to input accurate and up-to-date information to ensure the smooth exchange of insurance details in the event of an accident. Now that you've entered your personal details, you can generate your QR code for sharing. From the app's main screen, you'll find an option that says "Show QR Code." Tapping on this option will generate your personalized QR code, which contains your insurance information. To share your QR code, all you need to do is display it on your device's screen to the other party involved in the accident. They can then scan your QR code with their device to securely receive your insurance details.
